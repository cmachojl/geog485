What barriers to acquiring and processing the needed data do you anticipate?

Anytime you are downloading data I'm always prepared for the worst. Often times the data you think you are downloading is what you receive. For example, I downloaded a .shp file from gis.ny.gov that was supposed to be the boundaries for the different neighborhoods in New York and it was but the only had number associated with the boundaries which was less than helpful. Another problem is too much information in the attribute table and you have to modify the data for your needs which can be time consuming.
